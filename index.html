The Story

Hello! We are TreeOfLove. Been pursuing Aquascaping hobby for more than 10 years. Combining research with hands-on experimentation to create aquascapes. Through this site, We seek to showcase our works of experimentation and exploration.

​A little about us. When Education is revered with a strong focus on math and science... fields that were supposed to produce tangible, economically useful output. But thankfully, the biology knowledge gained in school came in useful in later years in figuring out what makes the ecosystem in a planted tank tick.

We find Aquascaping to be a nice blend of science and art. As the plants grow, they change the landscape, so it's a piece of art that looks different day to day. And, that is what separates planted tanks from static visual art displays. It is also a great opportunity to observe and appreciate the intricate working of natural systems - how tiny bacteria can be so important for tank cycling and contribute so much to overall tank stability. Anyone who has tried balancing an enclosed ecosystem will have a great appreciation of how the natural world works, which arguably is just a larger ecosystem enclosed by space.

The upkeep of planted tanks also require a certain amount of discipline and consistency. The same concepts apply to personal growth - improvement requires consistent, applied, conscious effort.
